03  /  Pipeline

The triage pipeline

Every customer has a triage deal in HubSpot. Move them through stages as you have conversations. You don't need to touch HubSpot directly, Claude can handle it.

PENDING
No outreach yet. Customer hasn't been contacted about the decline.
IN COMMS
You've reached out or they've come to you. They know something's up.
CALL BOOKED
A specific call or meeting is scheduled to discuss.
ACTIVE DISCUSSION
Back and forth. Customer is processing the situation.
RESOLVED
Conversation closed. Customer is satisfied or outcome is final.

Priority

ASAP
Needs immediate proactive comms.
THIS WEEK
Action required in the next few days.
NEXT WEEK
On the radar but not urgent.
NO ACTION REQUIRED
No outreach needed right now.

Agency triage pipeline

Agencies have their own pipeline: Pending → Notified → In Discussion → Resolved. When an agency moves forward, all brand champs with clients under that agency get notified automatically.

04  /  Data

Triage deal fields

All customer accounts and their respective agencies have been loaded. Here's how key fields are defined that will support with triaging.

Pro tip Not sure what data you can access or how a metric is defined? Simply ask Claude.

Data impact

Only available for Cohort 1 (tracking start date 1 October 2025 or before)

Severity & confidence

  • Severity high/medium/low/negligible
    Derived from the Predicted Awareness Change. Compares Oct'25 awareness to Nov'25–Feb'26, adjusting for brand size and geography. Internal indication only, final imputed results may vary. Severity can be assessed at Dashboard, Primary Brand, or Account level. Dive deeper with the Funnel Decline Deep Dive Analyst project.
  • Predicted Awareness Change
    How much we expect Awareness to change in the imputed results for Cohort 1.
  • Prediction Confidence high/medium/low
    How reliable the Predicted Awareness Change estimate is, based on available data points.

Cohort classification

  • Cohort 1
    Started tracking on or before 1 Oct 2025. Has a pre-decline baseline.
  • Cohort 2
    Started tracking on or after 1 Nov 2025. No pre-decline baseline, nor a way to categorise impact for triaging.
  • Survey Start Date
    The date tracking originally began for this account.

Dashboard usage (Nov 2025–Feb 2026)

  • Has Logged In
    Whether anyone logged in during Nov 2025–Feb 2026.
  • Active Every Month
    Whether the customer had at least one login every month from Nov 2025 through Feb 2026.
  • Login Users
    List of who logged in during the impacted period.
  • Export Event Count
    Copy-as-image or download events.
  • Timeline Download Count
    Timeline-specific downloads, indicating deeper engagement with trend data over time.

Customer health

  • ARR
  • Healthscore 0-10
  • CSM Pulse healthy/concerning/poor
  • Renewal Likelihood
  • Renewal Date
  • Months as Customer

Agency

  • Agency Name
  • Agency Notified yes/no
  • Agency Confidence
  • Partner Manager
  • Agency Relationship Type

Escalation

  • Escalation Reason legal/commercial/product
  • Escalation Person
  • Escalation Context

CS Funnel Decline Plugin

For Brand Champions

Manage your customer conversations without ever opening HubSpot. Prep for calls, handle post-call admin, log learnings, and escalate when needed.

📋
/cs-pipeline
See all your deals by stage. Make ad-hoc updates right here: move stages, flag priority, update outcomes.
🎯
/cs-prep <customer>
Call brief with HubSpot data, last Attention call summary, email context and playbook talking points.
🤖
/cs-postcall <customer>
AI analyses your call, suggests updates, you approve. Updates HubSpot and logs learnings to Notion.
🚨
/cs-escalate <customer>
Flag for leadership. Updates HubSpot escalation fields and generates a pre-call brief to share with the escalation person.
📖
/cs-playbook [topic]

📊

Brand Champ & Partner Manager alignment

When either side notifies an agency, Claude automatically keeps the other side informed. No one gets caught off guard.

Brand Champ notifies an agency
1

Agency Notified checkbox updated on the customer triage deal in HubSpot, so the notification is logged against the right record.

2

The agency's Partner Manager pinged in #funnel-declines-triage-meeting-changelog so they know the agency is aware and can coordinate from the partnerships side.

Partner Manager notifies an agency
1

Agency Notified fields updated in HubSpot on the agency triage deal — date, channel, and status all logged.

2

Relevant BCs pinged in #funnel-declines-triage-meeting-changelog across all brands that agency manages, so they can reach their contacts before the agency does.
